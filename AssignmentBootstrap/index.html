1. Pet Listings and Search

Feature: Display available pets for adoption with detailed profiles. Functionality: List pets by species (e.g., dogs, cats), breed, age, size, and location. Implement search and filtering options to help users find specific types of pets based on preferences.

Pet Adoption Process

Provide adoption application forms for users to express interest in adopting a pet. Allow shelters/rescues to review applications, conduct background checks, and approve or deny adoption requests

Pet Profiles and Descriptions

Include photos, videos, and detailed descriptions for each pet to highlight their personality, behavior, and medical history. Enable shelters/rescues to update pet profiles as needed and mark pets as adopted once they find a home.

User Registration and Profiles

Enable users to register, log in, and create personalized profiles. Provide a dashboard for users to track their adoption applications, saved pets, and communication with shelters/rescues.

Communication and Notifications

Implement messaging or email notifications to notify users about the status of their adoption applications.Allow users to contact shelters/rescues directly with inquiries about pets or adoption procedures.

Admin Panel for Shelter/Rescue Management

Provide shelters/rescues with tools to manage pet listings, adoption applications, and user communications.Allow for updating pet status (available, pending adoption, adopted) and maintaining accurate records of adoptions.
